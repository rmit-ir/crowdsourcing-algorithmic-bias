Our Mission

By collecting COVID-19 data and sharing with qualified scientists, we are leading the charge for a cure. Our data will facilitate research studies, public health programs, and plasma donation drives.

Our Vision

We aim to accelerate scientific progress by providing high-quality data to those working to beat COVID-19. We’ll then use this infrastructure to aid in the rapid response to future public health crises. We can find the cure now and be prepared for what’s next.

 
 
 
Take the survey

WHAT we’re fighting

The world’s health professionals are working tirelessly against COVID-19. But without reliable data on how the virus affects different populations, the battle remains uphill.

HOW we’re fighting

We are collecting a large database of information on patients who have tested positive for COVID-19, and sharing it with qualified scientists around the world.

WHO we are

We’re STFH Data Consortium, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization composed of epidemiologists, doctors, software engineers, and public health experts working together to propel science forward.

Our Principles

 

OPEN SCIENCE

The faster we can collect data and the more broadly we can share it, the sooner we can bring an end to this pandemic.

RIGOROUS DATA COLLECTION

Our team of more than 20 public health experts is committed to collecting valid, reliable data to enable other scientists to push their coronavirus projects forward more quickly. 

GLOBAL CITIZENSHIP

The only way we’re getting through this is together. We’re doing our part by empowering you to do yours.
